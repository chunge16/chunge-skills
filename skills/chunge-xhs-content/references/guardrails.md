# Guardrails

Keep the output punchy without becoming spammy or unsafe.

## Must avoid

- fake scarcity
- exaggerated promises
- medical or financial guarantees
- insulting, manipulative, or shame-based tone
- keyword stuffing in title or hashtags
- turning the body into long paragraphs

## Prefer

- one clear takeaway
- short lines with clean rhythm
- natural spoken Chinese
- concrete scenes, benefits, or judgments
- hashtags people might actually search

## Copy safety

Before finishing, check:

- no code fences
- no markdown bullets unless requested
- no analysis mixed into the final copy
- no extra headings beyond `标题`, `正文`, and `话题` for single-version output
